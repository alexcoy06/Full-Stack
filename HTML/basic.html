The Most Recommended Anime By Alexander Coy

Top 3 Isekai

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

Corporate employee Mikami Satoru is randomly stabbed and finds himself reincarnated in an alternate world as a slime. Now known as Rimuru, he embarks on a mission to build a world that embraces all races.

Overlord

When a well-known MMORPG is set to be permanently shut down, veteran player Momonga chooses to stay logged in. As NPCs start developing their own personalities and consciousness, he decides to use his skills to become the game's new overlord.

Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World-

Natsuki Subaru, an average high school student, is heading home from the convenience store when he suddenly finds himself in a different world. Lost and bewildered in this unfamiliar place where he doesn't even know his way around, he encounters a beautiful girl with silver hair who offers him help. Grateful for her kindness and eager to repay her for rescuing him from his own confusion, Subaru agrees to assist her in searching for something she is looking for.

Iwakakeru -Sport Climbing Girls-

Sport climbing involves utilizing both physical strength and mental acuity to ascend walls. Kasahara Konomi, a puzzle enthusiast, unexpectedly stumbles upon her school's climbing wall, which appears to her like a vibrant, intricate puzzle. This serendipitous discovery sparks significant transformations in Konomi's life. Alongside her teammates from the Hanamiya Girls' High School Climbing Club, Konomi embarks on an exhilarating journey towards conquering new heights in this gripping tale of passion and determination!

Haikyu!!

Adapted from Haruichi Furudate's original Weekly Shonen Jump manga series, Haikyu!! is a slice-of-life sports anime centered on Shoyo Hinata's passion for volleyball. Inspired by a diminutive professional volleyball player, Hinata forms a volleyball team during his final year of middle school. However, their first tournament pits them against Tobio Kageyama, known as the "King of the Court," and his team, resulting in a heartbreaking loss. Determined to surpass Kageyama, Hinata enters high school and joins the volleyball team, only to discover that Tobio has also enrolled.
